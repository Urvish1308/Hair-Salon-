Bald Fade

Today, a disconnected undercut is one of the most popular hairstyles for daring gents. The cut features a longer length of hair on top and significantly shorter sides. Unlike taper and fade styles, the disconnected undercut forgoes any gradual change in length and opts for a bold contrast instead.
